Overview

Purpose

Do you like rafting? Have you never heard about it? Don't worry, we can explain it. Rafting is Basically floating and navigating down free rivers.You do it on a non-motorized water craft (a kind of boat) with a group of about 8 people. We know it seems to be dangerous, but an experienced instructor acompanies the group during all the adventure and they take care of everyone in the group. This website is here to Show you how it works and where you can have the best experience of your life!

Audience

Who are the target costumers? We target young people, not in age, but with the spirit of the youth. People who like to go outise, look up to the sky and know they will make it a good day. People open to new experiences, and Since they are more than 12 years old, no matter how old they are. Do you fit in this description?

What do they want? Well, this one is easy! They want an unforgettable experience. These people are full of normal days and not exciting adventures, they want to feel the blood in their veins again. And where's the best place to do that if not on a boat going down a river at high-speed?

What do they expect to find accessing the webstite? All the information one may need to register and book a seat on our next rafting day.

What are their need that aren't being met? People maybe want a straight or faster way to contact us or see a calendary with free seats.

How will the audience access this site? Nowadays everything is fast and needs to be practical. The majority of people have smartphones and hold them all the time their hands are free. This is how the audience will access this site. Am I right?
